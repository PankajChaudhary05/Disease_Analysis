How does it work?

  • Enter Data Input

    Personal Information:- Users provide basic details such as age, gender, family history of diabetes, and lifestyle factors (e.g., physical activity, diet).
    Medical History:-Users may also input relevant medical information, such as blood glucose levels (if known), blood pressure readings, and any other health conditions.
    Symptoms :-Users report any symptoms they're experiencing, such as excessive thirst, frequent urination, fatigue, or unexplained weight loss.

  • Symptom Assessment

    Questionnaire:- The system may guide users through a series of questions about their symptoms, their severity, and how long they've been present.
    AI Analysis :-The AI algorithm analyzes the provided data, identifying patterns that suggest a potential risk of diabetes or the presence of related symptoms.

  • Prediction & Recommendations

    Risk Assessment:-The system provides a prediction about the of diabetes or the potential for specific symptoms, often expressed as a percentage or risk score.
    Guidance:-Consulting a healthcare professional for further evaluation and testing.
